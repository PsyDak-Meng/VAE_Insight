How should you use this website.

  • March 29, 2023
  • Instruction
  • TEAM023

The VAE-Insight is an integrated website to provide users with complete information on vaccines and side effects as well as visualization of models and their result. Users can use this website in the following ways:

  1. By clicking the LOGIN button in the navigation bar, users can login their account or register a new account follow the instructions on the registration page.
    • The website pays great attention to the private information of users.
    • When a stranger is not logged in to any account, he can only view limited information in restriction mode. He can explore the relationship between the features and side effects of the vaccine through the FACTORS part. But, he cannot do anything about viewing, editing the information, and using the prediction function.
    • Ater login, users can use the prediction function to predict the side effects of the vaccine based on their own information.
  2. By clicking the PREDICTION button in the navigation bar, users can view, edit their own information about the vaccine. And, they can use the prediction function to predict the side effects of the vaccine based on their own information by click the PREDICTION button.
  3. By clicking the FACTORS button in the navigation bar, users can employ the visualization constructed by us to explore the in-depth relationship between the features and side effects of the vaccine.
    • Users can select the features and side effects they want to know about by clicking the dropdown list.
    • The heatmap plainly visualizes the correlation between the selected variables using a set of color gradients.
    • The Sankey diagram will show the correlation (causality) between them in the form of flows, and the width of the flows will intuitively reflect the size of the different links. Through the presentation of the Sankey diagram, the user can well assess his own situation regarding the risk of side effects, which is one of the main goals of the website.
